Pack contain 2 procedural materials for walls and tiles. Materials are PBR. Resolution is from 32x32 to 2048x2048.
All settings are on screenshots.
• 4 examles of wall material
• 4 examles of tile material

Settings for tiles:
• Intensity of roughness and metallic
• Intensity of damage and dirt
• Color for tiles and dirt (ground)
• Set your own pattern
Settings for wall:
• Intensity of roughness and metallic
• Intensity of damage and dirt
• Color for wall
